About

Juvar is an agile full-stack web developer working with different projects in National Telehealth Center and College of Medicine, both at the University of the Philippines Manila. He's also the Chief Technology Officer of Pivotal Peak Digital Health Solutions, a pioneer spin-off of U.P. Manila. Back in 2016 to 2018, he was the Creatives Manager then a Community Manager of Google Developer Group Philippines (GDGPH), a developer community program by Google that provides opportunities and share different Google technologies to local developers. Juvar holds a Bachelors Degree in Information Technology from the De La Salle University Dasmariñas, Cavite, Philippines.
